Chinese Student Association

Our Goals

As students studying abroad alone, we all experienced bewilderment and loss. Friends and helps are the basis of living and studying in a foreign country. With numerous international students and intense competitions in Diablo Valley College, information sharing is particularly important. While, compared with international students from other countries, we have a large number of Chinese students, but we still don't have a relevant cohesive organization. In this respect, we sincerely propose that we Chinese students build an information-sharing platform, so that we can help each other and progress together.


Our Missions

We are committed to building a cohesive information sharing and mutual aid group, which adhering trueness, mutual aid, and win-win situation as our tenet. Trueness - We provide all aspects of detailed information, and we keep reliable, useful as standards of selection and publishing. Mutual Aid - We adhere to the principle of mutual benefiting, sharing and opposing mercenary and selfishness. Win-win situation - we are committed to providing a platform of making friends and sharing same ambitions while achieving striving and furtherance together, in order to achieve our goals of transferring to our ideal universities.
